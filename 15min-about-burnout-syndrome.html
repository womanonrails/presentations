Occupational Burnout Syndrome

It is not an illnesses or a medical condition.

It can have an impact on health.

It is a group of symptoms, body reactions linked to long-term stress in work.

- WHO (World Health Organization)

- feel exhaustion, negativity, cynicism - irritation, anger - lack of energy related to your job - headaches, chest pain - problems with sleeping - no motivation
Notes: - Burnout Syndrome - comes no from IT - 1974 - Herbert Freudenberger - observation of volunteers - at a free clinic for drug addicts - state like depression
- many ways of stress - inside or outside - **Outside** - too many responsibilities - too high expectations - conflicts and communication problems - hard work conditions, mobbing - **Inside** - low self-confidence, impostor syndrome - fear of speaking up - problem with adjusting to changes
